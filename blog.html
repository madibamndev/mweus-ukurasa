Hanging At The Precipice

The scorching afternoon sun shined brightest, perhaps a sign of what was to come. While on one of my evening game drives, I came narrowly close to reciting my final vows. I quiver with fear in one hand as I celebrate in sheer jubilation on the other as I walk down memory lane, reminiscing on the events of that fateful day. The car came to a screeching halt. While looking around, hoping to see a Tembo (elephant). Lo and behold, like the saying from my mouth to God's ears, mother nature blessed me with more than I had bargained for. A young elephant cub approached silently from the bushes with the entire la Familia within touching distance to the young Tembo like a colony of ants.


Hurriedly as I looked down to get the camera, mother Tembo rushed in fast and furious. My human-sized eyes locked in with those of mother Tembo. Prompting her to move closer in a manner likely to suggest that in here she is King and I the humble servant. As is standard, this show might usually last for a fleeting moment. However, this was a rare occasion when the wheels of justice can sway the other way. At this juncture, I had to clutch on the hope that my time had not yet come. She trumpeted vigorously like a clarion call for backup as I cried and prayed as my life depended on it. In all honesty, looking at my circumstances playing, the hero was out of the question.


The stalemate persisted for what felt like an eternity. The car's engine kept running in a bid to disguise the scent of what was my human life. Young Tembo seemed to be getting a kick as she continued to play under the watchful eye of her beloved mother. Within the midst of our standoff, the entire herd surrounded the vehicle. Thus heightening the level of fear beyond the tipping point, a degree deemed beyond reproach. The trumpeting; shaking of the ground increased in frequency. The chilling echos as shrubs and twigs were being flattened in readiness for the final send-off. Just as all hope seemed lost, the herd felt pity and headed across the road. Alas, I escaped this harrowing ordeal and sighed in relief. Such a terrifying and yet pleasant encounter that felt like clinging on for dear life at finem moratorium.

Getting to know you

How well do you know yourself? A loaded question with the potential to spark a plethora of thoughts and ideas. As we are aware, life is full of surprises and disguises that can either uplift us or break us down in equal measure, depending on the kind of response we choose to adopt. Hence, having a solid spine that can enable you to withstand the storms and remain steadfast in triumphs is a valuable measure of one's character. Therefore, knowing oneself is about having the ability to understand your emotional, mental, spiritual and physical strengths as you navigate through life.


That said, there is no clear-cut method coveted as the panacea for knowing thyself. It is a journey that needs to start with us as an individual, and then along the way, we can connect to networks that we can work with us hand in hand. Thus, the significance of us being the port of entry is to hold ourselves accountable for the eventual success or failure. When the stakes are increasingly enormous in this noble undertaking, and the weight of it all is by enlarging dependent on you, the chances are that you will have to make it work.


What are some simple strategies that we can leverage in this pursuit? Being aware of the stimuli around us based on the content we constantly consume and social interactions we normalize is a notable place to start. The positive or negative has a substantial impact on the kind of response that we project back to others. It is, therefore, safe to say that our response to stimuli is greatly dependent on the affirmations that we condition ourselves. Consequently, it is paramount that we thoroughly sieve our impetus in our daily dealings. One has to be both retrospective and introspective consistently.

Settling down by a certain age

In society, there are certain milestones that one is constantly looking forward to accomplishing. E.g. settling down. An overarching question that comes to mind is, is there such a thing as the right age to settle down? Some vouch for between the mid-20s to late 20s as being the ideal age for settling down. Another suggestion with regards to settling down is the ages between the early 30s to the mid-30s. Of the propositions named above, is one proposal better than the other? What are the merits and demerits of either? Is there an exact age for one to settle down?


Those who support the view of settling down between the mid-20s to late 20s often advocate for it with great emphasis on children. In their support, one of their points of convergence is a biological argument. Their viewpoint is that settling down and conceiving this early offers the children a greater chance at having a healthier upbringing. The notion that favours young parents as having the requisite energy that is substantial in sustaining the lives of energetic kids is a noteworthy example quoted as a supporting argument for settling down early. Are you convinced with this argument?


On the other hand, we have those who feel that it is better to settle down during the early to mid-30s. There is a consensus that one tends to focus more on long-term projects and plans in their life within this age bracket. The 20s is a phase of discovery and exploration. Through this exploration place, one gets to know themselves introspectively. Focus your efforts on getting the essentials up and running until you feel ready enough to settle down. Ignore the pressures to settle down by a certain age. Instead, start a family when you can provide and take care of their needs.
