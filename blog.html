Culture

Culture: the customary beliefs, social forms, and material traits of a racial, religious, or social group.


Culture is a subject that is close to our hearts in more ways than one. Take for example you meet a complete stranger and within moments of your interaction you start discerning several things such as mostly likely place of origin and depending on the breadth of your knowledge of other cultures, you thereby take it a step further and draw parallels with a particular group of people, beliefs, clothing and so much more. These fruits culminate into elements that we identify as belonging to a culture. When I think about culture several points come to mind, such as: clothing, beliefs, music, food, dance, people, traditions, heritage, language, colour, lifestyle, religion, hobbies, pride, art and craft to name but a few. The way in which one expresses him or herself using one or a combination of the mediums named above, forms the basis of what can be classified as a culture.
